पाठ्यक्रमको विस्तृत विवरण (Course Details) :

प्रथम पत्र : बैकिङ्क, लेखा, वित्तीय विश्लेषण र आर्थिक नीति

First Paper: Banking, Accounting, Financial Analysis and Economic Policy

समुह (क) बैकिङ्क

  1. बैकिन अवधारणा र विकास
  2. नेपालमा बैंकिङ्ग विकास र हालको अवस्था एवं चुनौती (Banking Development in Nepal, Recent Status and challenges)
  3. तहगत बैंकिङ्ग प्रणाली (क, ख, ग, घ वर्गीकरण)
  4. ग्राहकमुखी बैंकिङ्ग सेवा
  5. ग्राहक संरक्षण सिद्धान्त (Client Protection Principle)
  6. ग्राहक पहिचान
  7. नेपालमा बैंकिङ्ग क्षेत्रका अवसर र चुनौतिहरु (Opportunities & Challenges of Banking Sector in Nepal)
  8. निक्षेपका प्रकार एवं परिचालन विधि (Types of Deposit and Mobilization Procedure)
  9. मुद्रा तथा पुँजी बजार, नेपालमा यसको विकास, वर्तमान अवस्था एवं चुनौतीहरु (Money / Capital Market, its Development, Present status and Challenges in Nepal)
  10. शाख श्रृजना र यसमा राष्ट्रिय बाणिज्य बैंकको भूमिका (Credit Creation and Role of Rastriya Banijya Bank)
  11. नेपाल राष्ट्र बैंकद्वारा बैंक तथा वित्तीय संस्थाहरुका लागि जारी गरिएका निर्देशनहरू (Regulations and Directives Issued by NRB for Commercial Banks)
  12. Merger, Acquisitions and Corporate Restructuring in Nepal: Recent Experience and Development

समुह (ख) बैंक तथा वित्तीय संस्था सम्बन्धी ऐन, नियम तथा नियमनहरु

  1. नेपाल राष्ट्र बैंक ऐन, २०५८ (Nepal Rastra Bank Act, 2058)
  2. बैंक तथा वित्तीय संस्था सम्बन्धी ऐन, २०७३ ( Bank and Financial Institution Act, 2073)
  3. बैंकिङ्ग कसूर तथा सजाय ऐन, २०६४ (Banking Offence and Punishment Act, 2064)
  4. सम्पत्ति शुद्धीकरण मनी लाउन्डरिङ्ग निवारण ऐन, २०६४ (Anti Money Laundering Act, 2064)
  5. विनिमेय अधिकारपत्र ऐन, २०३४ (Negotiable Instrument Act, 2034)
  6. कम्पनी ऐन, २०६३ (Company Act, 2063)
  7. आयकर ऐन, २०५८ र आयकर नियमावली, २०५९
  8. विद्युतीय कारोवार ऐन, २०६३
  9. सुरक्षित कारोवार ऐन

समुह (ग) लेखा तथा लेखापरीक्षण (Account and Auditing)

  1. Concept of Accounting
  2. Analysis of Financial Statements
  3. Profit Analysis
  4. वाह्य तथा आन्तरिक लेखापरीक्षण प्रणाली (External and Internal Audit System)
  5. आन्तरिक नियन्त्रण प्रणाली जोखिम व्यवस्थापन तथा विश्लेषण (Internal Control System/Risk Management & Analysis)
  6. Nepal Financial Reporting Standards (NFRS)
  7. BASEL Core Principles and Capital Adequacy Framework

समुह (घ) वित्तीय विश्लेषण तथा मूल्यांकन (Financial Analysis and Evaluation)

  1. परियोजना विश्लेषण तथा मूल्याइन (Project Analysis and Evaluation)
  2. Portfolio Management and Analysis
  3. बजेटका सिद्धान्त, प्रकार, महत्व र व्यवहारिकता Principles and types of Budgeting. its Importance and Practices.
  4. Cost benefit analysis.
  5. पूँजीको श्रोत र उपयोग (Sources and uses of Fund )
  6. Profitability and Liquidity Management, Assets and Liability Management, Lending Principles and its Management. Determination of Interest Rates.
  7. Business Environment Analysis SWOT, PEST and ETOP etc.

समुह (ङ)आर्थिक नीति (Economic Policy)

  1. वित्त नीति (Fiscal Policy)
  2. मौद्रिक नीति (Monetary Policy)
  3. वाणिज्य नीति (Commercial Policy)
  4. औद्योगिक नीति (Industrial Policy)
  5. नेपालमा योजनाबद्ध विकास (Planned Economic Development in Nepal )
  6. विश्वव्यापीकरण र नेपालको अर्थतन्त्रमा यसको प्रभाव (Globalization & its Impact on Nepalese Economy & Challenges.)

द्वितीय पत्र : अर्थ, मुद्रा, व्यवस्थापन, रणनीतिक योजना तथा समस्याको पहिचान र समाधान

Second Paper : Finance, Currency, Management, IT, Strategic Planning and Problem Identification and Solutions

  1. Economy and Money :
    1. National Income Accounting , GDP Measurement and GDP Computation in Nepal and its Problem,
    2. Macro Economic Indicators (Economic Growth & Price, Public Finance, Money & Banking)

    6. Money Supply : Definition, Factors affecting Money Supply, High Power Money and Money Multiplier.
    7. Inflation : Concept ,Causes , Measurement and Consequences, Tradeoff between Inflation and Unemployment.
    8. Bank Risks Management Including BASEL II and Related NRB Directives.
    9. Trade Finance : Letter of Credit(L/C), Letter of Guarantee etc.
    10. WTO, Nepal's Accession, Opportunity and Challenges
    11. Privatization, Recent developments and challenges in Nepal
    12. International Organizations - I.M.F. World Bank, A.D.B., I.F.C.

  2. Management :
    1. Principles of Management and its Functions.
    2. Management Of Commercial Bank
    3. Decision Making Process, Importance and Factors Affecting Decision Making Process.
    4. Importance of Information and Communication System in Management.
    5. Good Governance, Corporate Governance and Corporate Social Responsibility.
    6. Change Management & Factors Contributing to Change.
    7. Leadership and Contemporary Issue in Leadership.
